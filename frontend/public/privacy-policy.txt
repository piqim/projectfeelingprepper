FEELINGPREPPER — PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: June 14, 2026
Last Updated: June 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Mustaqim Bin Burhanuddin ("Developer," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use the FeelingPrepper mobile application ("Application," "App," or "FeelingPrepper").

By creating an account or using FeelingPrepper, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, do not use the application.

================================================================================
1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
================================================================================

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you create an account and use FeelingPrepper, we collect the following information:

  A. Account Information:
     - Username (3–5 characters, chosen by you — not your real name)
     - Email address
     - Password (stored only as a securely hashed value using bcrypt — we never store or transmit your plain-text password)
     - Notification preferences (enabled or disabled)

  B. Wellness Journal Content:
     - GRAPES daily tracker entries (which of the 6 behavioral activation categories — Gentle with self, Relaxation, Accomplishment, Pleasure, Exercise, Social — you logged each day)
     - Cognitive Triangle (CogTri) journal entries, which may include thoughts, emotions, and behaviors you voluntarily enter
     - Daily check-in history, streak data, and activity timestamps
     - Pet companion selection (Fish or Seal) and associated status data

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

We currently do not use third-party analytics trackers or advertising SDKs. The following technical information may be processed locally on your device or minimally on our servers to ensure proper app functionality:

  - Authentication tokens (JSON Web Tokens / JWTs) stored locally on your device
  - Network connectivity status, processed locally on your device and not transmitted to our servers
  - Basic server logs, such as API request timestamps and error logs, which are not tied to identifiable user content and are used solely for debugging and maintaining app reliability

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:
  - Precise or approximate location data
  - Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or hardware fingerprints
  - Biometric data of any kind
  - Payment or financial information (FeelingPrepper is free to use)
  - Contact lists or other data from your device
  - Data from third-party social media platforms or accounts
  - Voice, audio, or camera data

================================================================================
2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
================================================================================

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  a) Account Management: To create, authenticate, and manage your FeelingPrepper account;
  b) Service Delivery: To provide and operate the core features of FeelingPrepper, including the GRAPES tracker, Cognitive Triangle tool, streak tracking, analytics dashboard, and pet companion system;
  c) Personalization: To display your pet companion status, personal stats, and tailored daily summaries;
  d) Security and Authentication: To authenticate your identity securely using JWT-based authentication and to detect and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity;
  e) Notifications: To send wellness reminders or check-in prompts if you have enabled notifications (this feature is subject to future implementation and your explicit consent);
  f) Support: To respond to your support requests, feedback, or inquiries;
  g) Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable legal obligations, respond to lawful requests from authorities, and enforce our Terms and Conditions.

We do not use your information for advertising, behavioral profiling, or any purpose not described in this Privacy Policy.

================================================================================
3. SENSITIVE HEALTH INFORMATION
================================================================================

The wellness content you enter in FeelingPrepper — such as your Cognitive Triangle journal entries (thoughts, emotions, behaviors) and GRAPES logs — may constitute sensitive mental health information.

We treat this data with the highest level of care. This content is:
  - Stored securely in your account database
  - Not shared with third parties for any commercial purpose
  - Not used for advertising, research publications, or AI model training
  - Accessible only to you and to our minimal technical infrastructure for the purpose of displaying it back to you

IMPORTANT HIPAA NOTICE: FeelingPrepper is NOT a covered entity under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). Your data in FeelingPrepper is therefore not subject to HIPAA's legal protections. Do not use FeelingPrepper as your sole or primary repository for sensitive clinical health records, and do not use it as a substitute for care provided by a licensed healthcare professional.

================================================================================
4. HOW WE STORE AND PROTECT YOUR INFORMATION
================================================================================

4.1 Storage Location. Your account information and wellness data are stored on MongoDB Atlas, a cloud database service operated by MongoDB, Inc. Data may be stored on servers located in the United States or other jurisdictions operated by MongoDB Atlas.

4.2 Security Measures. We implement industry-standard technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:
  - Password hashing using bcrypt with appropriate cost factors
  - JWT-based session authentication with token expiration
  - HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data transmitted between your device and our servers
  - Access controls and authentication requirements for database access
  - Regular review of our security practices

4.3 No Absolute Security. While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data. If you become aware of any security breach affecting your account, please notify us immediately at mbinburhanuddin@ucsd.edu.

4.4 Data Retention. We retain your personal information and wellness data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion:
  - Your account information and all associated wellness data will be permanently deleted from our primary database
  - Some residual data may remain in encrypted database backups for up to 30 days before being fully overwritten or purged
  - After 30 days, your data will be completely removed from all our systems

6. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES
================================================================================

6.1 Access and Update. You may view and update your account information at any time through the Settings page within FeelingPrepper.

6.2 Data Export. FeelingPrepper provides a built-in data export feature accessible in Settings. You may use this feature at any time to download a copy of your wellness data in a portable format.

6.3 Account Deletion. You may permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time by navigating to Settings → Delete Account. This action is irreversible. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

6.4 Notification Preferences. You may enable or disable notification preferences at any time through Settings.
